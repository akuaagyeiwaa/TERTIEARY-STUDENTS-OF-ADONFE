About Us

The Tertiary Students of Adonfe association was established to unite all tertiary students from Adonfe, providing a platform for networking, mentorship, and community service. Our association aims to promote educational excellence, community development, and support for students from our hometown.

Our Mission

To promote academic excellence and professional development among tertiary students from Adonfe while fostering community development initiatives.

Our Vision

To be a leading student association that creates opportunities for growth, networking, and positive impact in our community.

Our Values

Excellence, Unity, Integrity, Service, and Community Development.

Membership
